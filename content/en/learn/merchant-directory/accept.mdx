# Accept DAI Today

## Why Accept DAI?

DAI is the world's largest decentralized stablecoin and the foundation of Decentralized Finance. While we have been blown away by the explosive emergence of innovative financial instruments which leverage the the benefits of DAI, we are just as excited about DAI as a stable store of value and peer to peer transaction medium for merchants.
